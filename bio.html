Ariel Luque resume bio

       I am a Cuban immigrant and first generation college student originally from Guantanamo, Cuba, and living in Miami, Florida. I decided I needed to change my environment, and ended up 1,800 miles away in the city of Medford, attending the Tufts University School of Engineering, where I am currently a part of the Society of Latino Engineers and Scientists, as well as the Association of Latin American Students. As of now I am working in the Center for STEM Diversity helping maintain their website, and will hopefully be working on a full redesign in the coming month.
